Treatments

Treatments & Therapies

A modern treatment plan is built from accurate staging, tumor biology, and patient goals — not one-size-fits-all protocols.

Chemotherapy Immunotherapy Targeted Therapy Hormonal Therapy Palliative Care

Cancer Types We Treat

Hover or tap a card to explore the treatment focus for each cancer type.

Lung Cancer

NSCLC/SCLC, biomarker testing guidance, immunotherapy and targeted therapy options.

Blood Cancers

Leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma with modern chemotherapy, targeted agents and transplant coordination.

Breast Cancer

HER2-positive, hormone-positive and triple-negative breast cancer treatment pathways.

GI Cancers

Colorectal, gastric, liver and pancreatic cancers with multidisciplinary planning.

Brain Tumors

Glioma, meningioma and metastatic brain tumors with neurosurgery and radiation oncology coordination.

GU Cancers

Prostate, bladder and kidney cancers using hormonal therapy, targeted therapy and immunotherapy.

Thyroid Cancer

Differentiated and anaplastic thyroid cancers with endocrine coordination and modern systemic treatment.

Unknown Primary

Cancer of unknown primary (CUP) evaluation with evidence-based treatment planning.

Therapies

Chemotherapy

Systemic treatment plans designed according to cancer type, stage and patient condition with side-effect prevention and monitoring.

Immunotherapy

Eligibility assessment and guidance for checkpoint inhibitors and combination regimens where clinically appropriate.

Targeted Therapy

Biomarker-guided treatment options focusing on safety, effectiveness and regular follow-up.

Hormonal Therapy

Personalized hormonal treatment for breast and prostate cancers with quality-of-life monitoring.

Palliative Care

Pain management, symptom relief, counseling support and comfort-focused care alongside active treatment.
