About the Doctor

Dr. Arham Khalid

Dr. Arham Khalid is one of Pakistan's most distinguished oncologists, combining international training with a deep commitment to local patient care. With fellowships from leading cancer institutes in the UK and USA, he brings global expertise to every consultation.

His approach integrates the latest in immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and personalized medicine — ensuring every patient receives care tailored to their unique biology and circumstances.

Beyond clinical work, Dr. Khalid is an active researcher and public educator, working to raise cancer awareness across Pakistan and reduce diagnosis delays.
